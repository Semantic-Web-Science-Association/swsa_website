SWSA sponsors a number of awards to recognize and honor the achievements of the members of the Semantic Web Community:

- **[SWSA Ten-Year Award]({{ "/awards/swsa-ten-year-award/" | relative_url }})** recognizes the highest impact papers from the ISWC proceedings ten years prior.
- **[SWSA Distinguished Dissertation Award]({{ "/awards/distinguished-dissertation-award/" | relative_url }})** recognizes the PhD dissertation from the previous year with the highest originality, significance, and impact.
- **[SWSA Distinguished Service Award]({{ "/awards/distinguished-service-award/" | relative_url }})** awarded to individuals for their distinguished service to the Semantic Web community.
