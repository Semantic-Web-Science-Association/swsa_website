We are pleased to announce the winners of the 2025 SWSA awards.

Bo Xiong wins the SWSA Distinguished Dissertation Award for their thesis on “[Geometric Relational Embeddings](https://elib.uni-stuttgart.de/bitstreams/8d21197d-d37e-438d-ae8c-ec0bd0b927a5/download)”. Bo will receive the award at ISWC 2025, which includes a complimentary registration to the conference, a certificate, and a € 1,000 payment.

Furthermore, the SWSA Ten Year Award goes to “[RDFox: A Highly-Scalable RDF Store](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-319-25010-6_1)”, by Yavor Nenov, Robert Piro, Boris Motik, Ian Horrocks, Zhe Wu, and Jay Banerjee, published at ISWC2015.

We'd like to thank all colleagues who have been involved in reviewing the applications.

Congratulations to the winners!
